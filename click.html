Click

Ever Wanted to Time Travel? Adam takes on this role and shows us exactly what we are thinking with time travel.

Starring Adam Sandler Released: June 23, 2006

"Michael Newman (Adam Sandler) seems to have it all but his wife, Donna (Kate Beckinsale), is increasingly frustrated by the amount of time he has to spend at work. Michael cannot find time to be at home until he meets an eccentric inventor (Christopher Walken) who gives him a universal remote that controls time. At first he happily skips the boring times until he realizes the remote is in control of his life and he learns to cherish all the precious moments with his family."

Adam Sandler really outperforms himself with this family comedy and shows you he can also do a more serious role. Although there was times of serious moments, I could count on Adam to crack a joke and lighten the mood.

Grossings

Click grossed $137.4 million in the United States and $100.3 million internationally, for a worldwide gross of $240.7 million.
